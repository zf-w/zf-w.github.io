The Dream of Red Chambers

This is, I would say, the greatest literature among books I have read so far. I don't know the true academic ways of analyzing literature. However, I feel that the author depicted the characters in a way that allowed me to constantly have a new understanding of them and be empathetic to their feelings and actions.

I also enjoy the design of using characters' poems to implicitly reveal the characters' personalities and their fates. I deeply appreciate the author's careful, considerate, and sensitive mind that captured the warm and interesting details of life and souls.

The mindsets and philosophies behind the literature are profound and thought-provoking, for example, "The skillful work and the wise worry." I think one theme of the novel is that people will always be trapped by the things they are good at, including their personalities, skills, and mindsets.
